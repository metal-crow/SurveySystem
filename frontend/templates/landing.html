Tool Share

No need to spend a fortune on a tool that you'll only need to use a few times. Sign up for ToolShare now to begin borrowing the tools that you need to finish your project, and sharing tools that you own to help others finish theirs.

Login Sign Up

  • Sign up for ToolShare

    Start by clicking the Sign Up button in the top-left corner of this page. You will then be asked to fill out a small form to create your user account. After this quick process you will be ready to start using ToolShare!

  • Search for Tools

    After creating your account and logging into the site, you will have access to a list of tools that you are able to borrow near your location. Just search or browse through this list to find the tool you're looking for!

  • Borrow a Tool

    Once you've found the tool you want, just click the borrow button next to it and a request will be sent to the owner of the tool. After the owner approves your request you will be able to setup a meeting time to pick up the tool!

  • Share Your Tools

    Give back to the community by offering to share any tools that you own. This allows ToolShare to be a thriving community of people with similar interests.
